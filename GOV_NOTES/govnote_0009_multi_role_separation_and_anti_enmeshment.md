## 2. Core Principle: â€œFunction, Not Fusionâ€

Every unit (human or AI) operates by **function**, never by identity mixing.

- A contributor is a contributor.

## 5. Multi-Role Conflict Prevention

### **5.1 AI Cannot Hold Multiple Roles**
For safety:
